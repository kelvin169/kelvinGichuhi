01 — About Me
Engineer at the intersection of hardware & intelligence.

I'm a firmware and industrial automation engineer passionate about building robust, real-time systems that power critical infrastructure — from factory floors to IoT edge devices.

My work spans the full stack of embedded systems: writing bare-metal C for microcontrollers, designing PLC programs for industrial control, and architecting IIoT pipelines that bridge physical machines to cloud analytics.

I believe in open engineering — sharing knowledge, publishing code, and contributing to a community of builders who move the physical world forward.

This portfolio is my living record of that work: projects, write-ups, and experiments at the edge of firmware and industrial automation.
